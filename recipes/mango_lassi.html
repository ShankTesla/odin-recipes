Mango Lassi

Description

Mango Lassi is a popular lassi variety made with Indian yogurt, ripe sweet mangoes and cardamoms. A small amount of milk or fresh cream known as malai is also added for a creamy and rich feel. This also helps balance the sourness of yogurt. Sometimes ingredients like rose water and saffron are also added for a flavor-twist

A well made Mango Lassi is smooth, creamy and thick, yet of pouring consistency. It has a significant mango flavor, tastes sweet with delicate hints of sourness from yogurt.

Ingredients

  • 2 mangos - peeled, seeded and diced
  • 2 cups plain yogurt
  • Â½ cup white sugar
  • 1 cup ice

Steps

  1. In a blender, combine mangos, yogurt, sugar and ice.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour into glasses and serve.
